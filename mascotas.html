01 — The furry family

Meet my companions.

Cat

Luna 🌙

2 years · Female

Luna is a tabby cat with amber eyes who came to our home on a rainy December day. She is the undisputed queen of the household: demands affection on her own terms and has a special talent for appearing exactly when you're working on something important. Her favorite hobby is watching birds from the window and judging everything I do.

  • Independendent
  • Curious
  • Moody
Dog

Mango 🥭

6 months · Male

Mango is a golden retriever with infinite energy and unconditional love to give. He arrived as a puppy and since then has turned every corner of the house into his personal park. He is the most social in the family: makes friends in seconds and never rejects a walk, a ball or a nap next to you on the sofa.

  • Playful
  • Devoted
  • Mischievous
Rabbit

Perla 🤍

1 year · Female

Perla is the cutest surprise my niece brought me for my birthday. She is a small white rabbit and an enormous personality. She loves to explore, jump on the cushions and convince everyone that she deserves more carrots. She is the quietest in the family, but also the most observant.

  • Calm
  • Explorer
  • Gluttonous
02 — Fun Facts

What i learned from them.

  • 🐱

    Cats sleep between 12 and 16 hours a day. Luna is prove of this.

  • 🐶

    Golden retrievers were originally bred to retrieve things without damaging it, hence their "soft" mouth.

  • 🐇

    Rabbits are crepuscular animals: they are most active at dawn and dusk.

  • ❤️

    Having pets reduces stress and improves cardiovascular health. My cardiologist is proud.

🐾 Luna, Mango & Perla aprove this message.
